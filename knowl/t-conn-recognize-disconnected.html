Lemma2.10

Let \(X\) be a space. Then \(X\)is not connected if and only if it is possible to find open subsets \(U\) and \(V\) of \(X\) such that

  1. \(U \cap V = \varnothing\)
  2. \(U \cup V = X\)
  3. Neither of \(U\) and \(V\) is empty.

Such a pair of open sets is sometimes called a separation of \(X\).
