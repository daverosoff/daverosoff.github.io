Example3.B.7Determine Slope Given Two Points

Determine the slope and describe the line.

Determine the slope between \((3,1)\) and \((-2,1)\text{.}\) First identify \(x_1, y_1, x_2, y_2\text{.}\)

\begin{align*} (x_1, y_1)\amp =(3,1)\text{ and}\\ (x_2,y_2)\amp =(-2,1) \amp\amp\text{Use slope formula \(m=\dfrac{y_2-y_1}{x_2-x_1}\)}\\ m\amp =\dfrac{1-1}{-2-3}\amp\amp \text{Simplify}\\ m\amp =\dfrac{0}{-5} \amp\amp \text{Reduce}\\ m\amp =0\text{, horizontal} \amp\amp \text{Our Solution}\checkmark \end{align*}
