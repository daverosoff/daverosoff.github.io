Example1.C.29Division with Scientific Notation

\begin{align*} \dfrac{2.014\times 10^{-3}}{3.8\times 10^{-7}} \amp\amp\amp \text{Deal with front numbers and \(10\)'s separately}\\ \dfrac{2.014}{3.8}=0.53 \amp\amp\amp \text{Divide numbers}\\ 0.53=5.3\times 10^{-1} \amp\amp\amp \text{Convert this number into scientific notation}\\ \dfrac{10^{-1} 10^{-3}}{ 10^{-7}}=10^{3} \amp\amp\amp \text{Use product and quotient rule,}\\ \text{ }\amp\amp\amp \text{using \(10^{-1}\) from the conversion}\\ \text{ }\amp\amp\amp \text{Be careful with signs:}\\ \text{ }\amp\amp\amp (-1)+(-3)-(-7)=(-1)+(-3)+7=7-4=3\\ 5.3 \times 10^{3} \amp\amp\amp \text{Our Solution}\checkmark \end{align*}
