Example3.A.11Determine \(Q\)-Intercept

Find the \(Q\)-intercept of \(2a+5Q=8\text{.}\) In this case, let the vertical axis be given by \(Q\text{.}\)

\begin{align*} 2a+5Q\amp =8\amp\amp\text{ \(Q\)-intercepts have an \(a\)-coordinate of \(0\): set \(a=0\)}\\ 2(0)+5Q\amp =8\amp\amp\text{Simplify}\\ 5Q\amp =8\amp\amp\text{Divide both sides by \(5\)}\\ Q\amp =\dfrac{8}{5}\amp\amp\text{We have found the \(Q\)-value of the \(Q\)-intercept}\\ \amp\left(0, \dfrac{8}{5}\right)\amp\amp\text{Our Solution}\checkmark \end{align*}

The \(Q\)-intercept \(\left(0, \frac{8}{5}\right)\) is labeled on the graph of the equation.
