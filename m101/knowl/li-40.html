Item1

"Remove" the absolute value

  • If \(\lvert \text{expression}\rvert \lt p\) is less than a positive number make a three part inequality: \(-p\lt \text{expression}\lt p\)
  • If \(\lvert \text{expression}\rvert \gt p\) is greater than a positive number make an OR inequality: \(\text{expression}\lt -p\) or \(\text{expression}\gt p\)
