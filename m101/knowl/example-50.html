Example1.B.8Decimals on the Number Line

Determine the location of the decimals on the number line.

\begin{align*} 2.5=\amp 2+0.5\\ =\amp 2+\dfrac{50}{100}\\ =\amp 2+\dfrac{1}{2} \end{align*}

is a number half-way between \(2\) and \(3.\)

\(0.84=\dfrac{84}{100}\) is more than \(0.80=\dfrac{80}{100}\) but less than \(0.90=\dfrac{90}{100}\) which means it is a number between \(0.8\) and \(0.9.\checkmark\)
