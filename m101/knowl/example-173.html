Example3.B.4Determine Slope Given Two Points

Determine the slope and describe the line.

Determine the slope between \((-4, 3)\) and \((2,-9)\text{.}\) First identify \(x_1, y_1, x_2, y_2\text{.}\)

\begin{align*} (x_1, y_1)\amp =(-4, 3)\text{ and}\\ (x_2,y_2)\amp =(2,-9) \amp\amp\text{Use slope formula \(m=\dfrac{y_2-y_1}{x_2-x_1}\)}\\ m\amp =\dfrac{-9-3}{2-(-4)} \amp\amp \text{Simplify}\\ m\amp =\dfrac{-12}{6} \amp\amp \text{Reduce}\\ m\amp =-2\lt 0\text{, falls} \amp\amp \text{Our Solution}\checkmark \end{align*}
