Scientific notation is written as the product of two numbers:

  1. A number between one and ten (it can be equal to one, but not ten), and
  2. Ten to some power or exponent.
\begin{gather} \text{Scientific Notation: } a\times 10^b\text{ where }1\leq a\lt 10\tag{1.C.7} \end{gather} in-context
