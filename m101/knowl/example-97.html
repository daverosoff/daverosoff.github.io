Example2.A.11Eliminate Denominators

Solve for each variable.

\begin{align*} \frac{x}{-7}\amp =-2\\ (-7)\frac{x}{-7}\amp =-2(-7)\\ x\amp=14 \end{align*}

\begin{align*} \frac{t}{8}\amp =5\\ (8)\frac{t}{8}\amp =5(8)\\ t\amp =40 \end{align*}

\begin{align*} \frac{w}{-4}\amp =9\\ (-4)\frac{w}{-4}\amp =9(-4)\\ w\amp =-36 \end{align*}
