Example3.A.13Investigate Equation Relationships Graphically

Consider the graph of \(y=\lvert x+1\rvert\) in red together with the line \(y=3\) in blue below. For what values of \(x\) does \(\lvert x+1\rvert =3\text{?}\)

Using the graph, we see the red \(y=\lvert x+1\rvert\) and blue line \(y=3\) intersect at the two points \((-4,3)\) and \((2,3)\text{.}\)

Thus, \(\lvert x+1\rvert =3\) at \(x=-4\) or \(x=2.\checkmark\)
