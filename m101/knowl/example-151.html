Example2.C.17Computed Weighted Average Grade

Suppose your MAT-101 final course grades are as follows: Attendance and Participation \(=95\text{,}\) Unit Exams \(=83\) and Final Exam \(=72\text{.}\) Compute your overall weighted average in MAT 101.

\begin{align*} 95(.05)+ 83(.20) + 72(.75)\amp\amp\amp\text{Multiply each grade by its corresponding weight}\\ \amp\amp\amp PE\underline{M}DAS:\text{ Multiplication before Addition}\\ 4.75+16.6+54\amp\amp\amp PEMD\underline{A}S:\text{ Add}\\ 75.35\amp\amp\amp\text{Our Solution:} \end{align*}

Your overall weighted average in MAT-101 would be \(75.35\%\text{.}\) The Final Exam is given the highest weight so has the greatest influence on the final weighted average.\(\checkmark\)
